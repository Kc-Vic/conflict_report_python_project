About CivIntel

Empowering citizens to make their communities safer and more transparent

Our Mission

CivIntel empowers everyday citizens to make their communities safer, more transparent, and better informed. We believe that everyone should have the tools to document incidents, share concerns, and connect with the right authorities or organizations—safely and responsibly.

What We Do

  • Report incidents with written details
  • Geotag locations for accurate incident mapping
  • Send reports to relevant agencies or community groups
  • Track updates on your submitted reports

Why It Matters

Every day, important events go unreported because witnesses do not have a safe or accessible way to share what they see. We are changing that by giving people a direct voice in community safety, public accountability, and disaster response.

Our Principles

  • Safety First — Your personal information is always protected.
  • Transparency — Reports are timestamped, location-stamped, and securely stored.
  • Community Empowerment — We believe information belongs to the people who live it.

Get Started

Check out the feeds above to see what is happening in your community. Want to share something? Log in securely or sign up to start reporting.
